Friends is one of the most iconic sitcoms of all time, and it has left an indelible mark on pop culture. Introduction Friends is a hit American sitcom created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, that aired from 1994 to 2004 for a total of ten seasons. It stared Jennifer Aniston (Rachel Green), Courtney Cox (Monica Geller), Lisa Kudrow (Phoebe Buffay), Matt Le Blanc (Joey Tribbiani), Matthew Perry (Chandler Bing), and David Schwimmer (Ross Geller).
